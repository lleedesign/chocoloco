We're not crazy,
just crazy about chocolate

Handcrafted in St. Louis!

There's a reason why Chocoloco is known for serving up the finest truffles in St. Louis. Every lick of our decadent creations is handcrafted using only the finest quality, 100% all-natural ingredients.

We start with the purest of creams and stir in flavors from some of St. Louis's most loved treats. Finally, we sprinkle in lots of love to make melt-in-your-mouth creamy treats for anyone who's feeling like a sweet treat.

Every day, we churn up sweet, truffle combinations including vegan and non-dairy options. Get the 411 on what flavors we're currently serving up or stop in to see what's tempting today!

A little love goes a long way in today's world. At Chocoloco, we're all about creativity, compassion and community. We try to do our part by being mindful and intentional about what goes into our truffles and how we create each tasty treat.
