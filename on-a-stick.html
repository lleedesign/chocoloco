Yes, treats ...On A Stick!

Mallows

Mallows with Choco-truffles, of course! They are great ways to celebrate the gushiest day of the year, dunking mallows in choco-late and dusting them with heart-shaped sprinkles can be just as fun. We'll have your next holiday covered. Mallow Pops are fun and delicious snack for any age (note "snack" and not "dessert," which means it's appropriate to eat them anytime after 9 a.m.). Craving a savory treat? We have salted pretzel rod options!

Crispies

Chocolate Dipped Rice Krispie Treats elevate an afternoon snack to a decadent dessert that will be perfect to serve at any party. We turned these choco-coated marshmallow crips treats into a lollipop bartopped with your pick of sprinkles, candies, or nuts.

Choco-pops!

Cake pops are fun, but do you know what's even better? Truffle pops. I know, right!? Maybe we're biased, but we're not wrong. These are great giveaways for parties or cute little treats to have around the house. Lot of ways to customize these: pick your flavor, dip, sprikle, color....all of it!
